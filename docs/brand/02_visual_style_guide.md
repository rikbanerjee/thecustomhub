## 4. Photography & Mockups

- **Style:** Clean, bright, and aspirational.
- **Background:** Use consistent, neutral backgrounds (light gray, off-white, or subtle textures).
- **Composition:** Products should be the hero. Use lifestyle shots that feel authentic and relatable, but maintain a polished, professional look. Avoid busy or distracting backgrounds.
- **Consistency:** All product mockups should follow a consistent template for lighting, angle, and scale.
